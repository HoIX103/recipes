Ingredients:
1. 2-4	EGGS
2. Butter
3. Salt and pepper

Steps:
1. For Over-Easy or Over-Hard Eggs: HEAT 2 tsp. butter in nonstick
   skillet over medium-high heat until hot.

2. BREAK eggs and SLIP into pan, 1 at a time. IMMEDIATELY reduce heat to
   low.

3. COOK SLOWLY until whites are completely set and yolks begin to thicken 
   but are not hard. SLIDE turner under each egg and carefully FLIP it 
   over in pan. COOK second side to desired doneness. SPRINKLE with salt
   and pepper. SERVE immediately.
